Chocolate Chip Cookie's

Not typically soft and doughy-but instead thin, crispy deliciously chewy.And extremelly difficult to eat just one

Yield:
18-20 cookies
Preep time:
20 minutes
Cook-time:
11 minutes

Nutritional information

Serving size : 1 cookie

68

3.5 g

Ingridients

  • 7 tbsp soy-free margarine
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup cane sugar
  • 1 flax egg:1 tbsp ground flax mixed with 3 tbsp water
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tspbaking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cupoat flour
  • 1 cup almond Flour
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 and line a baking sheet with parchmant paper
  2. Mix together the flax,egg and set aside
  3. With an electric mixer ,beat the margarine and oil until fluffy
  4. Add the sugars and beat for another 1-2 minutes untill crime
  5. Beat in the flax egg & remaining ingridients
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips
  7. Shape balls of dough and place them on the baking sheet
  8. Bake for about 10-11 minutes ,untill slightly golden around the edges
  9. Allow them to cool for 5-10 minutes on the baking sheet and then transfer to a cooling rack for another 10 minutes
